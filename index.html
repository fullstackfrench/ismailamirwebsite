Programs

Objective

Ismail engages with students and professionals in 60-120 minute in person and virtual experiences in which he helps participants connect with their purpose in order to ignite their ability to move through their pain towards their destiny through storytelling, personal reflection and activities designed to help participants recognize their own limitless potential.

Topics

For Students

Everything I’m Not Makes Me Everything iAM

This presentation is about Purpose. Ismail empowers students to take a hard honest look at some the most challenging experiences of their lives, helping them to gain the necessary insight required to embrace those challenges and utilize them as stepping stones. Ismail will helps students recognize how their “struggle” is preparing them for who they are destined to become.

Built Different

Ismail inspires students to realize the power of Diversity. Students connect to explore their own identities, uncover bias, learn how to leverage the diversity in their environments and discover the benefit to seeking unfamiliar perspectives.

Facts Over Feelings

In this session, Ismail helps students understand the power of Emotional Intelligence. Students learn to step outside of their feelings, to think instead of react, and to utilize their feelings as tools instead of being used by them.

G.R.O.W. Through What you Go Through

Students will leave this presentation equipped with the skills to overcome personal and academic challenges by walking through Ismail’s acronym for the word GROW.

MEET ISMAIL AMIR

Ismail Amir is a motivational speaker, author, and educational consultant. He is trusted by educators, students and corporations to share components of Social Emotional Learning through his compelling life story to help students and professionals learn to shift the way they look at adversity in order to ‘Grow Through What You Go Through.’ Ismail has facilitated workshops and trainings for numerous schools and organizations like The City of Philadelphia’s Office of Children & Families, The Community College of Philadelphia, Microsoft and The Pennsylvania Institute of CPA’s to help shape the minds of the current and future leaders of tomorrow by identifying obstacles holding them back from being average and leaning into their gifts to build the life of success they desire. However, Ismail was not always a driven and inspirational servant leader.

"Ismail Amir is a motivational speaker, author, and educational consultant."

Ismail learned to expect to lose at a very young age. First, losing mother just two weeks before his sixth birthday. Then, the death of his father just one week after his eleventh birthday. By the time he was a teenager, struggling both academically and socially, Ismail made up in his mind that his life was purposeless. Lacking a sense of direction Ismail wandered into his twenties focused on drinking, partying, and trying to escape the void he felt from what he had lost as a child. It wasn’t until his time working for Apple when his eyes began to open to the power he harnessed through his pain. Ismail recognized his uncanny ability to ignite a spark in others who were blind to the possibilities that their own live’s held.

"Ismail recognized his uncanny ability to ignite a spark in others who were blind to the possibilities that their own live’s held."

"Today, Ismail has the family he always wanted as a devoted husband and father of two."

Today, Ismail has the family he always wanted as a devoted husband and father of two. He is driven by his passion for helping other's like him, "those programmed to lose", to embrace their past and drive them to their dream future. He believes that there is a purpose in the pain and everyone's story is worth embracing. He encourages his audience to recognize that despite loss of loved ones, the environment they grew up in, trauma experienced or feelings of unworthiness, ‘We were not born to be average. We were born to be different, born to be you!’
